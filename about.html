ABOUT SFHS




Mission

SFHS is committed to produce quality education to all
through relevant curriculum realized by dedicated
teachers who are technically competent and as shepherd
of the students- mold their minds, hearts and souls
for the glory of God.
Vision

SFHS- a school of excellence. envisioning
to produce individuals with deep sense of nationalism,
who are God fearing, globally competitive and embraced
with positive attitudes that will enable them
who seek the truth.
